Founders

Sumit Pal Singh is a male nutrition expert based in India who has gained a reputation for his expertise in the field of nutrition and dietetics. With a focus on evidence-based nutrition and personalized counseling, Sumit has helped countless individuals achieve their health and fitness goals through targeted nutrition interventions and education.

Sumit understands the unique nutritional needs of athletes and the role that specific nutrients play in optimizing their physical performance, reducing the risk of injury, and supporting recovery.
